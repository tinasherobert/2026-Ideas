# Lay-By: Save Now, Buy Later (SNBL)

**Idea #3** — Digital lay-by service alongside the core (e.g. ROSCA service).

Customers reserve goods or services and pay over time (no credit check, no interest); merchants get higher conversion and basket size. Reuses wallet identity and, where possible, recurring-payment rails.

---

## 1. How it sits with the core

| Core | This service (Lay-By / SNBL) |
|------|------------------------------|
| **ROSCA service** — group savings, rotating payouts, collateral | **Lay-By** — individual “reserve at merchant, pay in instalments”; fulfilment when plan completes |
| Same **wallet / identity** | Same **wallet / identity**; single login, unified history |
| Group-based, member-driven | Merchant-driven plans; deposit + weekly/monthly instalments |
| No credit, interest-free | No credit, interest-free (aligned positioning) |

One brand, one wallet: **savings groups (ROSCA service)** and **pay-over-time at merchants (Lay-By)** as complementary products.

---

## 2. Services (from LayUp-style investigation)

### Consumer-facing

- **Save Now, Buy Later** — Reserve an item or service with a **deposit**; choose a **plan** (weekly or monthly, payment day where allowed); pay **instalments** until complete; then **fulfilment** per merchant policy (classic lay-by).
- **No credit check, no interest** — Positioned as debt-free, instant approval, low friction.
- **Omnichannel** — Lay-By at **online checkout** and, where feasible, **in-store / POS** (“plan anywhere”).

### Merchant-facing (B2B)

- **Lay-by as a service** — APIs and/or **ecommerce plugins** (e.g. Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento).
- **Variable Recurring Payments (VRP)** — Automated collection of instalments; fewer failed payments and less manual chasing.
- **Merchant visibility** — Dashboards for plan status, completion rates, and basic reporting (optionally: drop-off reasons, cohort retention, product-level performance).
- **Refunds, cancellations, substitutions** — First-class flows for changes and stock issues.

### Verticals beyond general retail

- **Travel** — “Book now, pay later” for holidays (deposit + instalments; fulfil on payment complete).
- **Pre-orders** — Reserve not-yet-available items with a plan (deposit + schedule).

### Optional growth layers

- **Rewards / loyalty** — Network-level rewards for on-time payments and plan completion (e.g. BOOST-style); repeat use across ROSCA service and Lay-By.
- **B2B recurring beyond lay-by** — Same VRP/recurring rails for **subscriptions or memberships** (gyms, education, insurance-like).
- **Merchant “growth stack”** — Abandoned-cart recovery for plan shoppers, segmentation, promos (e.g. “pay deposit today, lock price”).

---

## 3. How AI can support this service

- **Affordability guidance** — Recommend instalment amount or plan length from behaviour (no credit bureau); reduce checkout drop-off.
- **Predictive reminders** — Best channel/time/message (e.g. WhatsApp, email, SMS) to improve on-time payment and completion.
- **Plan “rescue”** — When a payment is missed, offer structured options (e.g. extend by 2 weeks, smaller next instalment) to keep the customer in good standing.
- **Merchant intelligence** — Which products sell more on lay-by, optimal deposit and plan length; feed into merchant dashboards.
- **Fraud / anomaly detection** — Suspicious signups, payment anomalies, merchant abuse; keeps risk and ops under control without becoming a credit provider.

---

## 4. Differentiation vs BNPL and others

- **Not BNPL** — No “take now, pay later”; fulfilment only when the plan is paid (or per merchant policy). Clear “save then take” / no new debt message.
- **Not a credit provider** — No credit checks, no interest; aligns with debt-free positioning.
- **Same wallet as ROSCA service** — One identity for group savings and merchant lay-by; potential for rewards across both.

---

## 5. Summary

A ** Lay-By (Save Now, Buy Later)** service that:

1. Runs **alongside** the core (ROSCA service) with shared wallet and brand.
2. Reuses **consumer and merchant** patterns from the LayUp-style investigation: deposit + instalments, VRP, no credit/interest, omnichannel, plugins, dashboards, travel/pre-orders.
3. Can later add **rewards**, **B2B recurring**, **merchant growth stack**, and **AI** (affordability, reminders, rescue, insights, fraud) to grow the business.
