# Businesses: AI support chat and call bots

**Idea #2** — AI-powered chat and voice bots for business customer support.

This service provides businesses with AI-driven support agents that handle customer inquiries via **chat** and **voice (calls)**. Bots answer FAQs, triage issues, collect context, and escalate to humans when needed—reducing wait times and support load while keeping a consistent, 24/7 presence.

---

## 1. Scope

| Area | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Channels** | Chat (web, in-app, messaging) and voice (inbound/outbound calls) |
| **Audience** | Small to mid-size businesses that want automated support without a full contact centre |
| **Integration** | APIs and/or no-code/low-code setup so businesses can plug into existing help desks, CRMs, or websites |

---

## 2. Possible features

- **Unified bot platform** — One AI model/config for both chat and call; consistent tone and knowledge base.
- **Knowledge base** — Train on business docs, FAQs, and past tickets; keep answers accurate and on-brand.
- **Handoff to humans** — Escalate to live agents with full context (transcript, intent, customer info).
- **Analytics** — Volume, resolution rate, escalation rate, and satisfaction (CSAT) per channel.
- **Customisation** — Brand voice, languages, and business-specific flows (e.g. returns, bookings, billing).
